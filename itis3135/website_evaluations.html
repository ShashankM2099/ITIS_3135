Website Evaluations

World's Worst Website Ever

This is the world's worst website because the site does not comply with the principles of CRAP. CRAP stands for Contrast, Repetition, Alignment and Proximity.

  • Principles of CRAP
  • There are four principles when it comes to CRAP
  • First, it starts with C- stands for Contrast
  • Reading the elements in the site, the site has too many colors and hard to focus on one single element.
  • Second, comes to R-epetition
  • Most of the fonts in the WebSIte are repeated throughout. Makes it a sore for eyes, and the same goes for colors
  • Next is A-lignment
  • Texts, Images, Links are all linked to either side of the page, and some of the texts are half-linked.
  • Last is Proximity
  • When you scroll down, there is a lot of whitespace, just scorlling down, gets bored.

Lowe's Store

Now this is a website that follows the principles of CRAP

  • Principles of CRAP
  • There are IV principles when it comes to CRAP
  • First, it starts with C- stands for Contrast
  • Lowe's store uses blue and white mostly paying homage to their store's primary colors
  • In contrast, the site does not do rule-bending when it comes C in CRAP.
  • Second, comes to R-epetition
  • When it comes to Repetition, the site does not repeat elements, most of the elements are organized differently, and made sure they feel new and feel something different.
  • Next is A-lignment
  • Lowes store uses tables and rows, but you cannot see rows and tables, and they are organized correctly for the user
  • Last is Proximity
  • When you open the page, you can see everything is proximal range and does not veer of their course
